Including Instructions in templates

You can also include Instructions in other pages, to make sure your participants do not forget what to do or what the rules are.

In order to to this, you have to:

  1. Create the page of instructions to be inserted (here we chose "Text.html")
  2. Create a block "include", by writing {+% include "name_app/name_page_to_include.html" %+}, without the "+" symbols (for us, it will be: "mock_app/Text.html")
